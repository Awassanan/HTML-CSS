  • Bubble counter algorithm

    Oct - Nov 2022

    This group term project, a component of the Image Processing course, aimed to utilize OpenCV and our acquired knowledge to accurately count the number of bubbles in a test tube. We encountered various challenges with each image, such as colorful backgrounds, watermarks, black and white color schemes, and blurry images. Primarily, I applied the Canny edge detection algorithm, masking with bitwise-and, and median blur techniques to eliminate irrelevant regions. Additionally, I employed thresholding, dilation to connect dashed lines, and utilized the built-in function findCountour from cv2 to accurately count the bubbles within the images.
